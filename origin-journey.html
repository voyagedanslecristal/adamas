THE ASCENT

From the mantle's depth,
a violent grace

Kimberlite eruptions — rare geological events — carried diamonds from their birthplace 150 kilometres below the surface to the Earth's crust at extraordinary speed. Without this violent ascent, no diamond would ever reach human hands. It is the rarest of geological accidents. And it happened for yours.
